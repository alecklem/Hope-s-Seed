Miracle Model of the Month





Hannah Faith was born with a partially inverted duplication on the 18th chromosome. She is the only known one with this. As her Dr stated her legacy will forever live. Hannah's life expectancy is unknown. At first, we're told she wouldn't live 24 hours, maybe a year, then 2. Hannah has many so struggles but still remains to smile. She shows me daily how thankful she is. I was blessed enough to be her Mommy. With that being said I too have had many battles and lots of fights for her, her needs and her rights! Hannah taught me so much about life. No matter how tuff I believed life was, Hannah's smile would always remind me how beautiful life is. I believed in unconditional love, how strong I was and how life could never knock me down. How to never take the word "No" from anyone. How to stand my ground to advocate for her and every "specially abled" child. Hannah made me a soldier, how to stand strong knowing I could overcome any battle in life. Hannah brought so much peace and love into my life, though we had a battle of some sort every day. I was able to love, adore, kiss, hug, and cherish the angle God blessed me with. The doctors gave Hannah hours to live, Hanna gave her family 17 years. Hanna has gone to heaven, however, her story is not over.
