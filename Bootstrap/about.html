About Hope's Seed


Hope's Seed Purpose is:

To inspire families of children with special needs to know that there is hope in every situation
To support, educate, and encourage families of children
To promote a philosophy of normalcy for children with special needs
To educate medical professionals regarding the importance of support for families
To enrich the lives of children
To connect communities and families with special needs children in order to promote understanding
To empower families to look outside of their own issues in order to support other families with special needs children
